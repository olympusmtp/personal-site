ABOUT ME

I am a commissioner/requester, with a low cost gallery. I'll request artists of all types, as I love seeing artists interpretations of characters I own. I rarely give explicit instruction on requests, leaving artists with open instruction for their own artwork. Although I don't spent exorbitant funds on artwork, I do try to respect artists as much as possible, and understand prioritizing work. I am patient.

I'm a monolingual native english speaker, but I can simplify my vocabulary or use a translator upon request.
